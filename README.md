### ***Description***
#### A marketplace designed to sell used and new gaming products with a twist that helps sellers to better price their products.
***

### ***Technologies Used***
* HTML 5
* CSS
* Technology 3
* Mongo DB
* Express API
* EJS
* Mongoose
* Method-override
* Morgan
* Dot Env
***

### ***Getting Started***

##### Guests can visit the site to view products but once they are ready to buy, then they can sign up and sign in to view the preferred contact details of the sellers.


##### Sellers can Sign up and sign in as a new user and then begin viewing the different sections of the website and list their products.
